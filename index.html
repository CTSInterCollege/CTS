About

C.T.S Inter College is reffered as Chandravati Thansingh Inter College. C.T.S Inter College is founded for farming the virtues of Nationality, Character and Personality Development in 2016 by the outstanding effort of Shri Abanish Kumar Yadav by taking the insipiration from his mother Smt. Chandravati and in sweet memories his lovely father Late Shri Thansingh Yadav. C.T.S is located at Patiyali Highway in Kadarganj. In the year 2017, affliation was given by Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad from Class 6th to High School . The school believes in imparting knowledge/values ​​and nurturing the talent, enthusiasm and creativity of its students to achieve excellence through high quality educational endeavors.
